Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04.03.2025

1. Data Controller

The data controller is F4ntom Games ("we," "us," "our company"). For privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at: support@f4ntomgames.dev.

2. Data Collected

Our mobile games collect data solely for statistical and marketing purposes, including:

  • Information on how the game is used (e.g., playtime, progress, interactions),
  • Device identifier (anonymous if provided by the SDK),
  • Data on game performance and potential errors.

We do not collect any personal data that could directly identify users.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected data is used for:

  • Statistical analysis to improve game quality and functionality,
  • Monitoring application stability and performance,
  • Displaying personalized ads.

4. Third-Party Services

Our games use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Unity (game engine) – Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) – Privacy Policy
  • GameAnalytics (data analytics) – Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

Collected data is stored exclusively by the above-mentioned third-party services and is not stored on our own servers.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Our games may use cookies or other local storage methods to save game settings and player progress. No personal data is stored.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their data,
  • Request correction of their data (if applicable),
  • Restrict or object to data processing.

Since we do not store any personally identifiable information and all collected data is anonymized and processed by third-party services, users do not have the right to request data deletion.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: support@f4ntomgames.dev.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be published in this section. Continued use of our games after an update signifies acceptance of the new terms.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@f4ntomgames.dev.
